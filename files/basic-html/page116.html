           Simon’s son, Ed, wants to learn to drive. He is fifteen years old.

           Ed:     Dad, I want to get my driver’s license.
           Simon:   You have to get a learner’s permit first.

           Ed:     How do I get that?
           Simon:   I can help you with the rules. But in this state, drivers
                   under the age of eighteen have to take a driver’s
                   education class at school. It’s the law. You have to
                   study thirty hours in the classroom.

           Ed:     A class takes a long time. I can learn faster with you.
